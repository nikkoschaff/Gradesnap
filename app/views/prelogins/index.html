Grade your tests

Print bubble sheets, scan them with any conventional scanner, upload them here. We'll grade them in seconds. Our scan sheets are free for any member of Gradsnap.

We allow you to utilize all resources available to you. Making use of scanners and scrap paper is what Gradesnap is all about. Saving money, saving time.

Does the busy work

Compiles full statistics, down to the most-frequently chosen wrong answer for each question. We understand that grading a test takes time.

However the issue doesn't necessarily lie in the grading. It's the statistics that you need from that grade. We create statistics for you. No manual input.
